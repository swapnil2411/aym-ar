Our Journey at a Glance

Today, we enjoy the patronage of thousands of customers using several unique and diversified products developed by us. But the journey of our ascent begins with our inception 37 years ago.

1984

Company listed on the Bombay Stock Exchange

1985

Launch of yarn dyeing and texturising facility in Palghar, India

1992

POY, FDY spinning and texturising facility launched at Silvassa, India

2000

Commercialised dope dyeing in India

2004

Manufacture of polyester mother yarn and mono yarn first time in India

2012

India's pioneering manufacturer and first merchant exporter of BCF yarns

2013

Established R&D centre for excellence and innovation

2015

Company's name changed to AYM Syntex Limited

2018

Expanded BCF yarn production capacity by additional 50%

2020

Commissioning of new plant at Naroli

2022

Highest ever annual revenues and margins in FY 21-22
